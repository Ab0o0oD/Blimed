Our Facility

We are located in the four-star Que Santa Kanjo Hotel. We house seven treatment rooms accommodating our massage and aesthetic services. We also have eight pedicure/manicure chairs which allow us to accommodate group bookings and wedding parties.

We have a Relaxation Room and Swiss Showers, as well as a room especially for couples. Food and wine orders are also available to guests with their spa experience. To provide you, our valued guest, with the most rejuvenating experience for your mind, your body and your spirit so your whole self is in balance.

Our Staff

All therapists and aestheticians at Alberta Massage and Spa are fully trained and registered. Our therapists are trained from a variety of backgrounds. We encourage our guests to book treatments with a variety of therapists to experience different techniques.

If you have any concerns with a service, we advise you to be open and communicate with your Therapist at the time of your treatment. If we don’t know, we cannot try and find a solution. We are proud to be the largest spa in Medicine Hat and area, offering the most extensive menu of services.
